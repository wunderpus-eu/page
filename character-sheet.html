An improved character sheet for DnD 5e

Faithful to the original, but featuring many additional fields and trackers, more space for notes, and a clearer layout. Fillable with rich text and ready to print. Perfect for 2014 and 2024 rules.

Download A4 Download Letter
For best results, print with "Fit to paper" or "Scale to fit".

Combat-ready design

Complete status bar with:

  • Resistances & immunities
  • Hit point dice tracker
  • Conditions & exhaustion tracker
  • Line for death saving throw notes like Advantage

Ability scores with corresponding skills, featuring:

  • Proficiency & Expertise and a line for short notes
  • Additional stats: carrying capacity, age, height, weight

Improved core stats:

  • Line for Initiative notes like Advantage
  • Additional speeds like climbing or swimming
  • Long & high jump
  • Additional senses like blindsight or tremorsense

More lines for weapons, more love for martials:

  • Equipment Training includes weapon masteries
  • Checkboxes for main hand and off-hand
  • Ammunition tracker

Better class overview with more space:

  • Save DC and tracker for main class resource
  • Round counter

More space for loot & magic

Larger appearance & backstory sections, including:

  • Field to add a portrait
  • Section for languages
Use Acrobat Reader to add a portrait PDF (must be cut to a circle with transparent background).

Larger equipment section with:

  • Consumable trackers
  • Space for non-attuned magic items

More space for spells, more love for casters:

  • Line for number of prepared spells
  • Better-arranged spell slot trackers
  • Checkboxes for prepared spells
  • Checkboxes for required & consumed material components
  • Trackers for free spellcasts
